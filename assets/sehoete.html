Sehoete

The rantipole (Daucus carota subsp. Sativus) ke motso meroho, hangata lamunu ka 'mala, le hoja pherese, e ntšo, e khubelu, e tšoeu, le dikhalthiba bosehla teng. Lihoete ke liphoofolo tse ruuoang foromo ea rantipole hlaha, Daucus carota, letsoalloa ho Europe le boroa-bophirimela ho Asia
b>Semela Type: moroho
Letsatsi Kopana: feletseng Sun
Mofuta oa mobu: leha e le efe
Mobu ph: Hanyenyane asiti ho se nke lehlakore
Phepo e nepahetseng
The rantipole fumana bath lona, e khanyang 'mala oa lamunu, ho tloha β-carotene, 'me palo e monyenyane ea α-carotene, γ-carotene, lutein le zeaxanthin. α- le β-carotenes ba karolo e 'ngoe metabolized ka vithamine A
Peo
Lihoete ka ho bolokoa bakeng sa likhoeli tse 'maloa ka ka sehatsetsing kapa ka morung mariha ka le mongobo, pholileng sebaka. Bakeng sa polokelo e telele e tlang
