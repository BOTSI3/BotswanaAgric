Litapole Monate

The litapole monate (Ipomoea batatas) ke dicotyledonous dimela hore ke ea hoseng e be khanya lelapa Convolvulaceae. Lona kgolo, starchy, monate-latsoeheng, le metso tuberous ke motso meroho
Semela Type: moroho
Letsatsi Kopana: feletseng Sun
Mofuta oa mobu: leha e le efe
Mobu ph: Hanyenyane asiti ho se nke lehlakore
Temo
The dimela ha e mamella serame. E e ntse e hōla molemo karolelano mocheso oa 24 ° C (75 ° F), khanya ea letsatsi le ngata le masiu a futhumetseng rainfalls selemo le selemo oa 750-1,000 limilimithara (30-39 ka) li nkoa ho fetisisa loketse
Dikahare phepo
Ntle starches bonolo, e tala litapole monate ruileng rarahaneng lik'habohaedreite, le motsoako faeba le beta-carotene (A provitamin carotenoid A), ha a ntse a ho ba le litaba tse ka hare e leka-lekaneng ea micronutrients tse ling, ho akarelletsa le vithamine B5, vithamine B6 le mankanese (tafole).
