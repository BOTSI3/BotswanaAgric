Morara

morara A ke litholoana, botanically le monokotsoai, tsa deciduous Woody lifate tsa morara tsa lipalesa semela genus Vitis. Morara ka jeoa a hloekileng e le morara tafole kapa ba ka sebelisoa bakeng sa ho etsa veine, jeme, lero, jelly, morara peo tlhotla, morara o omisitsoeng, asene, le morara peo oli. Morara ke mofuta bao e seng climacteric tsa litholoana, ka kakaretso bo etsahalang masihla
Semela Type: moroho
Letsatsi Kopana: feletseng Sun
Mofuta oa mobu: leha e le efe
