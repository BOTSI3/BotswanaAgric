Namunu

The lamunu ke litholoana tsa mefuta dilamunu Citrus × sinensis ka lapeng Rutaceae. [1] E boetse e bitsoa lamunu monate, ho khetholla ho tloha Citrus amanang × aurantium, a bua ka 'mala oa lamunu baba.
Semela Type: moroho
Letsatsi Kopana: feletseng Sun
Mofuta oa mobu: leha e le efe
Mobu ph: Hanyenyane asiti ho se nke lehlakore
Climate
Joaloka limela tse ngata dilamunu, dilamunu etsa hantle tlasa itekanetseng thempereichara e-pakeng 15,5 le 29 ° C (59.9 le 84,2 ° F) -le hloka tjhelete e khōlō ea mahlaseli a letsatsi le metsi.
kotulo
Sekhurumetso-sisinya bakotuli phetha molao ho sebelisoa ka ho eketsehileng ka Florida ho dilamunu kotula.
